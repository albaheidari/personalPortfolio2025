ABOUT

A dedicated Sound Designer and Music Practitioner with a decade of practical experience in both the Music and Live Events industries across Europe. Working as an Audio Editor in Colourful Radio, where I recorded, edited and mixed interviews, shows and music to broadcast quality. Alongside this, I have been actively involved in live events, including work with Fold London, gaining strong technical knowledge of production workflows and event organisation. Combining my creative problem-solving skills with adaptive audio systems, these opportunities have resulted in frequent, close collaborations with other artists, writers, and developers to craft immersive soundscapes and emotionally resonant sonic experiences. My recently completed BA (Hons.) in Music and Sound Design allowed me to further my exploration of how people connect with sound on an emotional, physical and social level, whilst instilling in me the necessary practical skills to support my career, including a professional knowledge of Pro Tools and Logic Pro.
I am an open and adaptable collaborator with strong social skills and an ability to work confidently with new people and in diverse teams. I am curious, empathetic and hardworking.
